Valen - Currency Management App Privacy Policy

Welcome to Valen, the currency management app that helps you stay disciplined with your money. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

Valen may collect the following types of information:

  • Financial data, including account balances and transactions, to provide you with currency management tools and insights.
  • Contact information, such as your email address, to send you important updates, notifications, and support responses.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with personalized currency management tools and features to help you track and manage your finances effectively.
  • To send you important app updates, notifications, and relevant information to enhance your user experience.
  • To respond to your inquiries, feedback, or support requests.
  • To improve our app's functionality and user experience through analytical insights.

Data Security

Valen takes the security of your personal information seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services and APIs to enhance our app's functionality and provide you with the best possible experience. These third-party services may collect and process your data as outlined in their respective privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies for more information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or through app notifications.

Contact Us
